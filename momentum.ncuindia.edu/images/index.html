MOMENTUM '17

Momentum is our annual techno-cultural fest where we provide mass and you are the velocity. Spanning over three days, the fest has a plethora of activities and competitions. From the brain storming sessions in quizzes and debates to exhilarating events like fashion show and histrionics, Momentum has it all! The fashion show is an exhibit of enthralling performances by handsome hunks and beautiful dames of various colleges. From the ethnic wear to western, our models scorch the ramp with utmost elan and grace. Momentum also hosts cultural programmes such as dance competitions, solo and duet songs, dramatics, best rangoli, jam sessions, X-Fame and many more. The War of DJs and Star Night are highly appreciated and enjoyed immensely by all. We invite you to be a part of this extravaganza on the the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd of November 2017. Mark the dates!.

With bigger hopes and better expectations, MOMENTUM, ’17 comes to you with the sweetest theme of the “Good Old 90s”, an era we all wish we can go back to.
